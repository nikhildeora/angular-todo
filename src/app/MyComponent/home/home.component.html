Angular Todo by Nikhil Deora

Are you looking for an elegant and feature-rich todo app? Look no further! This meticulously crafted todo app is designed to streamline your task management, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable experience.

Core Angular Concepts:

Our todo app leverages various core Angular concepts, showcasing the power and flexibility of this robust framework. Key Angular elements incorporated into this app include:

  • Directives: We utilize Angular directives to enhance the user interface, providing dynamic interactions and intuitive user experiences.
  • Decorators: Angular decorators are used to enrich the functionality of our components, ensuring a clean and maintainable codebase.
  • Custom Pipes: We've implemented custom pipes to enhance the presentation of your todo items, making them more informative and visually appealing.
  • Form Module: With the Angular Form Module, you can effortlessly add new todos. Our app enforces mandatory fields, ensuring that each todo is complete and meaningful.
  • Routing: Angular routing enables seamless navigation between different sections of the app, making it easy to manage your todos and explore their details.
  • Dynamic Routing: We've implemented dynamic routing, allowing you to access in-depth information about each todo item with a simple click.

Task Management at Its Best:

Our todo app provides an efficient and organized way to manage your tasks. It automatically sorts todos based on their deadline dates, ensuring that the most urgent tasks appear at the top of your list. If a task's deadline is within two days or less, it is highlighted in red, accompanied by an "urgent" label, making it impossible to miss important deadlines.

Deadlines with Precision:

Our app lets you set deadlines for your todos, but with a unique twist. You can only select the current date or a date in the future, ensuring that you stay focused on the tasks at hand and avoid backlogging.
